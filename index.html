ABOUT THE GAME

Welcome to The Grand Marketplace!

Build your wealth by buying low, selling high, and utilizing your Forge to combine basic resources into enchanted treasures.

How to play:

• My Hub: Manage your inventory and Forge items. Gather 9 identical items of any tier to forge them into a single upgraded item.

• Markets: Trade with visiting merchants. Completing a deal will put that slot on a 10-second restocking cooldown. Shuffle the market manually if you seek fresh opportunities!

• Upgrades: Unlock new rare items, expand your warehouse stack limits, and increase your concurrent deal slots.
